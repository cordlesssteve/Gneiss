Project 1 Gneiss
This will be the name we reserve for the original concept.

Core Mechanic A 2D, hex-grid, cross-section of a mountain.

Gameplay You are an amorphous, evolving rock entity that grows by absorbing adjacent terrain. It's a highly tactical, turn-based roguelike focused on positioning and survival, like a tower defense of one.

Inspirations Into the Breach, Slay the Spire.

Keywords Tactical, 2D, Roguelike, Defensive, Amorphous, Positional.
